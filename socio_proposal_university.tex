\section{About This Proposal}

This document presents a set of structured licensing options for the SOCIO platform — a fully production-deployed university event management system built by students of Christ University. The university may choose any of the options presented, or use them as a basis for further discussion.

Each option is designed to be mutually beneficial. We have structured these scenarios so that the university has clarity on exactly what they receive, what the costs are, and what the terms look like — without ambiguity.

\begin{infobox}
\textbf{What is being offered:} The right to deploy, operate, and — depending on the selected option — resell the SOCIO platform to other institutions. The platform is already live and serving real users at \texttt{socio.christuniversity.in}.
\end{infobox}

% ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
\section{What the University Receives}

Regardless of which licensing option is selected, the university receives:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item Full source code of all three products — socioweb (\texttt{socio2026v2}), socioapp (\texttt{sociomobilev2}), and sociogated (\texttt{universitygatedv2})
    \item Complete deployment documentation and handover support
    \item A working Android APK ready for distribution to students
    \item All third-party service configurations (Supabase, authentication, notifications, AI chatbot)
    \item A platform already proven in production at a real university — not a prototype
\end{itemize}

\section{Option A — Full Ownership Transfer}

\begin{scenariobox}[Full Ownership Transfer]{darkgray}
The university acquires complete intellectual property ownership of all three products. No ongoing obligations or fees of any kind after the transfer.
\end{scenariobox}

\vspace{0.4cm}

\subsection*{What the university receives}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, noitemsep]
    \item Full IP ownership of socioweb, socioapp, and sociogated

    \item No future obligations or dependencies on the original developers

\end{itemize}

    \item MCA fee waiver for both developers (₹4,80,000) — documented in contract

\section{Option D — Revenue Share}

\begin{scenariobox}[Revenue Share Model]{gold}
Lower upfront cost. The developers receive a fixed fee for every institution the university successfully onboards using the SOCIO platform. Both parties benefit when the product succeeds.
\end{scenariobox}

\subsection*{What the university receives}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, noitemsep]
    \item Full source code and deployment rights
    \item Right to resell to other universities

    \item Pay more only when you earn more — risk is shared
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{What the developers receive}

    \item Upfront cash payment
    \item ₹1,00,000 for each institution the university deploys the platform to

\section{Option E — Hybrid Model (Recommended)}

\begin{recommendbox}
\textbf{This is the option we recommend.} It balances a fair upfront payment with a modest per-institution fee that only applies when the university actively deploys to other institutions. The university benefits from a lower upfront cost; the developers participate in the platform's success. The SOCIO name is retained and travels with every deployment.
\end{recommendbox}

\vspace{0.6cm}

\begin{scenariobox}[Hybrid — Upfront + Per-Institution + Brand Retention]{green}
A one-time upfront payment, MCA fee waiver, SOCIO brand retention, and a fixed fee per institution the university deploys to. Clean, structured, fair to both sides.
\end{scenariobox}

\vspace{0.4cm}

\subsection*{What the university receives}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, noitemsep]
    \item Full source code of all three products with complete handover
    \item Exclusive rights to deploy and resell to other universities
    \item Full operational control of every deployment
    \item Lower upfront commitment than a full IP purchase
    \item A branded, recognisable product — ``Socio [University Name]'' — that builds institutional credibility as it scales
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{What the developers receive}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, noitemsep]
    \item Upfront cash payment
    \item MCA fee waiver for both developers — documented in contract
    \item ₹75,000 per institution the university deploys to
    \item SOCIO brand name retained — travels with every deployment
    \item Right to build new products under the SOCIO brand independently
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Pricing}

\bottomrule

\vspace{0.4cm}

\subsection*{Why This Works for the University}

If the university sells the platform to each institution at ₹3,00,000 per deployment (a conservative estimate for a bundled ERP module), a ₹75,000 per-deployment fee to the developers represents a 25\% margin cost — well within standard software distribution margins. The university retains ₹2,25,000 per institution after the developer fee.

\subsection*{Conditions}
\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*, noitemsep]
    \item All deployments to other institutions carry the ``Socio'' name as part of the product title
    \item University may not register ``SOCIO'' as a trademark
    \item Developer credit (Sachin Yadav and Surya Vamshi) acknowledged in product and documentation
    \item Non-compete is limited to this specific codebase — developers may build new independent products
    \item Deployment record shared every six months; per-institution fee due within 30 days of go-live
    \item MCA fee waiver is guaranteed for the full programme duration regardless of deployment outcomes
\end{enumerate}
