Out of Scope: No sociomobile work and no sociogated work

Legal Track No Coding Required Risk Reduction Priority

Detailed Legal Ticket Set (Extensive)

BHO-LEGAL-01

Master Legal Baseline and Version Ledger

Focus: Create one authoritative baseline for terms, privacy, and cookies language with version history and effective dates.

Input needed: Current policy text and founder-approved business rules.

Deliverable: "Policy Baseline v1" redline doc and a public-facing change log section text.

BHO-LEGAL-02

Consent Language Consistency Audit

Focus: Ensure all consent prompts use consistent legal wording and no contradictory obligations.

Input needed: Screenshots of all consent points from web flows.

Deliverable: Approved consent copy set with mandatory and optional consent distinctions.

BHO-LEGAL-03

PII Data Inventory and Legal Basis Mapping

Focus: Catalogue all personal fields collected and map each to purpose, legal basis, and retention requirement.

Input needed: Data dictionary and sample exported records provided by Advaith/Hatim.

Deliverable: Data Register spreadsheet with fields, sensitivity class, basis, owner, retention term.

BHO-LEGAL-04

Retention and Deletion Policy for Live Data

Focus: Define retention windows for users, registrations, contact messages, notifications, and attendance records.

Input needed: Current backup/storage policy and operational constraints from the engineering team.

Deliverable: Retention matrix and deletion request protocol (legal plus operational).

BHO-LEGAL-05

Outsider Flow Legal Guardrail Pack

Focus: Validate outsider profile flow, visitor ID usage, one-time name edit language, and misuse disclaimers.

Input needed: Outsider signup/profile screenshots and current warning messages.

Deliverable: Outsider policy annex and approved warning copy set.

BHO-LEGAL-06

Communications and Notification Compliance

Focus: Separate transactional vs informational notifications and define opt-out language where required.

Input needed: Sample notification messages and current trigger conditions.

Deliverable: Notification policy clause and outbound messaging compliance checklist.

BHO-LEGAL-07

DSAR and User Rights Operating SOP

Focus: Formal process for access, correction, deletion, and objection requests with SLA timings.

Input needed: Current support handling process and escalation owners.

Deliverable: DSAR SOP + response templates for support and legal escalation.

BHO-LEGAL-08

Cookies and Tracking Representation Validation

Focus: Verify cookie claims match actual implementation and remove outdated statements if unused.

Input needed: Browser cookie capture report from tech team plus current policy text.

Deliverable: Cookie disclosure truth-test report plus corrected policy copy.

BHO-LEGAL-09

Third-Party and Vendor Disclosure Schedule

Focus: Build legal disclosure list for all major processors and service dependencies used by SOCIOWEB.

Input needed: Vendor list from founders and infrastructure owners.

Deliverable: Vendor disclosure table + DPA required/not-required tracker.

BHO-LEGAL-10

Campus Event Participation Legal Terms Annex

Focus: Draft participant obligations, organizer responsibilities, conduct clauses, and dispute handling.

Input needed: Registration flow screenshots and founder policy decisions.

Deliverable: Terms Annex A for event registration and participation.

BHO-LEGAL-11

Contact Form and Support Inbox Legal Language

Focus: Add legal-safe text around response timelines, handling expectations, and sensitive info warnings.

Input needed: Current contact forms, support templates, and escalation process.

Deliverable: Updated contact disclosures and support messaging policy copy.

BHO-LEGAL-12

Legal Incident and Breach Escalation Playbook

Focus: Define severity tiers, notification thresholds, evidence logging requirements, and escalation matrix.

Input needed: Current incident response process from Hatim and founders.

Deliverable: Incident legal playbook with role ownership and response timeline.

BHO-LEGAL-13

Plain-Language Rewrite Pass for User-Facing Legal Pages

Focus: Keep legal correctness while improving readability for students and organizers.

Input needed: Existing policy text and approved legal baseline.

Deliverable: Readability-improved legal copy with no reduction in enforceability.

BHO-LEGAL-14

Final Compliance Sign-Off Bundle

Focus: Consolidate all outputs into one review package for founder approval and implementation handoff.

Input needed: Final outputs from all prior legal tickets plus founder comments.

Deliverable: Complete legal compliance bundle with risk register and final recommendation memo.

Milestones (Execution Order):
1) BHO-LEGAL-01 to 04 (policy and data baseline),
2) BHO-LEGAL-05 to 09 (operational controls and disclosures),
3) BHO-LEGAL-10 to 14 (commercial clauses, final playbooks, and sign-off pack).
Required output format: each ticket must produce at least one written artifact (redline, matrix, SOP, annex, or checklist) and one implementation recommendation note.
Review gate: no legal copy goes live without founder approval and cross-check against implemented behavior.
